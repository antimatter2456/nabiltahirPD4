Colourful Tic Tac Toe

I always wondered how it would feel to play the classic game of 'Tic-Tac-Toe' on a laptop/PC instead of mobile.

So, by making use of a function called 'bestmove', the AI program is made aware of the current game state, and it returns the best possible move it can make on the board to challenge the player

Also, by implementing a 'reset' button, the player is able to freely immerse themselves in the game, either to kill time, or for pure entertainment.
